About me

I am drawn to the mysteries of the universe, fascinated yet puzzled by what make the fundamental building blocks of existence, and the intricate relationships that bring our world to life. In my studies of Physics, I have come to appreciate the significance of the smallest components, from electrons to atoms to molecules and beyond. Through their interactions, we witness the emergence of beauty and life. This is why I chose the field of "Correlated Matters," where we play around, build and tune novel materials, creating mini-universes to investigate exotic fundamental phenomena.

Today, we face an exciting possibility to expand our intellect and perception, breaking the limit of physical forms. As both a physicist and a computer scientist, I feel incredibly excited to be presented with an array of cutting-edge technologies such as VR/AR, AI, Super Computers, and Quantum Computers. We can bridge the gap between the digital and physical worlds, and continue to establish new frontier. I am thrilled to be part of this journey of learning, growing, and exploring in these fields, and I am dedicated to design the future.
